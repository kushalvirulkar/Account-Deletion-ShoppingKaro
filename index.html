Account Deletion Request - ShoppingKaro

We are sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your account from the ShoppingKaro app, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the ShoppingKaro app on your device.
  2. Go to your account settings by clicking on your profile.
  3. In the settings, tap on the "Privacy Policy" option.
  4. Scroll down to the 28th section of the policy.
  5. There, you will see an option to "Click here to delete your account." Click on it.
  6. Once you click the option, you will be prompted to confirm your account deletion.
  7. Confirm the deletion, and your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

All data associated with your ShoppingKaro account, including your email address, will be permanently deleted.

Data Retention Policy:

After account deletion, no data will be retained. However, any data required for legal or security purposes may be retained for a limited time as per our privacy policy.
